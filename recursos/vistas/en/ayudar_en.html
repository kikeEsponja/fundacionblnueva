Institutional Objectives and Goals

Our challenges and goals focus on the professionalization and transparency of social work:

  • Achieve institutional self-sustainability in the short and medium term.
  • Transparency: Disseminate actions through digital media and accountability audits.
  • Generate a participatory, supportive, and transparent social culture.
  • Effectively attend to the vulnerable, abandoned population of the region.
  • Professionalize social action with highly qualified and committed personnel.
  • Establish a dynamic organizational structure for the achievement of altruistic purposes.
