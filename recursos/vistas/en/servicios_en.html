Our Projects

Betty Linares Foundation for the Elderly and Vulnerable Population

Our foundation has a portfolio of projects designed to address diverse needs and generate self-sustainability with tangible results.

01

Social Dining Rooms

We seek to guarantee access to nutritious food for elderly people and vulnerable families. We operate dining rooms in Chacahuayo and Cogri (Moquegua) with differentiated social menus (S/5 and S/6.50).

Investment: S/ 25,000 per dining room Self-sustainability: 14 months (Break-even point: 35 meals daily) Current Impact: 139 direct beneficiaries
02

Products Aqua Dignidad

Micro plant for packaging treated water (barrels, bottles, alkaline). Economic pillar to subsidize social programs with high quality standards.

"Every drop is a thread of support for our neighbors in need."

Investment: US$ 45,000 (machinery and infrastructure) Recover: 6 months Employment: 20 direct positions and hundreds indirect positions
03

Quiosco Preferente "Dignidad"

Economic Inclusion for elderly people through modular kiosks. Aligned with policies supporting elderly and vulnerable populations.

Investment: S/ 6,500 per modular kiosk Commitment: Free meals for 3 abandoned adults per kiosk Employment: 6 positions (direct and indirect) per unit
04

Party and Fair Solidarity

Social enterprise promoting itinerant shows (cuisine, fair, health). Total reinvestment of profits in benefit of vulnerable people.

Investment: S/ 520,000 (mobile infrastructure) Recover: 6 months Scope: 20 distrites from Arequipa Metropolitana
05

Senior Housing Chain

Chain of senior housing with integrated accommodation (54 rooms), gym, and specialized medical care in Cerro Colorado and other areas.

Target Population: 1.1M elderly people in poverty (18.14% national) Model: Public-Private Partnership Approach: Interdisciplinary and support network
06

Medical Post Chacahuayo

Implementation and valorization of a primary health center in a marginal frontier zone (Arequipa/Moquegua) for preventive and emergency care.

Investment: S/ 85,000 Annual Target: Serve 700 inhabitants in the first year Sustainability: 14 months

We work to provide quality of life to vulnerable populations, especially the elderly in a state of abandonment.
