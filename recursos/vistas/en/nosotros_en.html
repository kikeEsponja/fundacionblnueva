Our Foundation

The region of Arequipa, like much of the country, faces persistent challenges such as unemployment, economic precarity, underutilization of resources and deficiencies in public management. These problems create a scenario that demands creative and socially impactful responses. The Betty Linares Foundation emerges and strengthens precisely from this reality, grounding its action in the need to generate sustainable solutions.

With 14 years of experience in social action and successful ventures, we have developed expertise that allows us to socialize and expand our projects. We have the capacity to articulate efforts with people and companies that demonstrate detachment and solidarity with the most vulnerable social groups.

🎯

Mission

We work to meet the needs of our community, providing tools for their permanent and sustainable self-sufficiency. We offer services with quality and professional warmth, generating a positive and sustainable social impact that contributes to the well-being and dignity of our community.

👁️

Vision

We have consolidated as leaders in service activities, recognized not only for our superior business quality, but also for articulating efforts with people and entities públicas y privadas de espíritu altruista y filantrópico.

Core Values

Our pillar is Social Commitment and Sustainability. We operate as a private, non-profit organization, under principles of Integrity and Transparency, and always subject to the current laws and regulations, with supervision and control.

Foundation's Purpose

Our purpose is the integral protection and support of vulnerable populations that are abandoned, including all people who contribute to the purposes and objectives of the Foundation.

We work to provide and grant quality of life to vulnerable populations, especially the elderly in a state of abandonment.
